<b>Vid-iT</b> allows you to locate YouTube videos matching the music collection stored on your Android device.  Once you find videos that you like, you are able to add them to a "Favorites" list and replay them with a single click.  You can also locate and save favorite videos by searching with just a title and artist you enter by hand.  This simple and easy to use app works on smartphones and tablets.<br/>
